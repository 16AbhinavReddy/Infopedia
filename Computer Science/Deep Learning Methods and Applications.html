Deep Learning Methods and Applications

Microsoft researchers Li Deng and Dong Yu wrote this book. It provides an overview of deep learning methodologies and their application in a variety of signal and information processing tasks, such as automatic speech recognition (ASR), computer vision, language modeling, text processing, multimodal learning, and information retrieval.
