A history of the United State

A history of the United States by Cecil Chesterton is a very informative book. It is a must read for the history buffs and those interested in the history of nations and the US in particular. This book mainly concentrates extensively on topics like the English colonies, the mantle of Washington, the spoils of Mexico and on the slavery.
