The Civilization of India

The Civilization of India is a journey through the past from Vedic period to the times of Maratta ascendency,the book vividly describes the civilization of the land through ages.India of yore--it's people and rulers--comes alive in these pages. This work has been selected by scholars as being culturally important, and is part of the knowledge base of civilization as we know it.
