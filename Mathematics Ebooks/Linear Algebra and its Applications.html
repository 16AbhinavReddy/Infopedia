Linear Algebra and its Applications

Theory and problems of linear algebra has been designed to cater to the need of students opting linear algebra as a subject at undergraduate and postgraduate levels in various Indian universities. The book exhaustively covers the subject matter and its applications in various fields. To understand the subject matter covered in the book, reader must be aware of some basic Concepts of abstract Algebra. These prerequisites have been covered in chapter-0.
