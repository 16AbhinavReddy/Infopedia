Introduction to Electrodynamics

The book is a very useful comprehensive textbooks for students who are studying Electrodynamics. Divided into numerous sections, this book comprises of certain useful discussions on experiments, theory and history pertaining to Electrodynamics. The book is kept concise with the main points being clearly highlighted in every chapter. Unlike other books, students do not just get formulae in this book; instead, they also get detailed explanations and are warned of pitfalls, too. The book has been written by David J. Griffiths, who is a very respected name in the field of Electrodynamics.
